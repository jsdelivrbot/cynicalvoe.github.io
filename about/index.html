About Me

My name is Haden, better known across the Internet as FireBreath15. I am currently a college student studying Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. As an ISTJ, I spend my free time working on code, listening to music, reading books, watching anime, and playing games.

I've been working with code since I was 12 years old, and I have been addicted ever since. Starting out with using GameMaker, I sold my first program to a friend for $0.75 when I was 13. A little annoyed that my parents wouldn't let me charge more than $1.00, I was still excited to create things others wanted to buy. Following this, I built a free time-management program for my friends in high school.

It wasn't until 2012 that I delved into the recesses of Minecraft modding, and taught myself how to use Java. Some of my most popular and favorite projects written for Minecraft can be found on my Portfolio page for more information. Today, I still spend my free time with the Minecraft community by writing Bukkit / Spigot plugins, and I love every minute of it. When I'm not working on a Minecraft project, I can be found hanging out with Tinkerbell (she's my Raspberry Pi), or working as a Server Support Specialist at CubedHost.com.
