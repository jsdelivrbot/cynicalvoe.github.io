About Me

My name is Haden, better known across the Internet as xFyreWolfx or FireBreath15. I am currently a college student studying Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. When I'm not studying, I spend most of my time programming, playing games, and with my friends. I have been working with code since I was 12 years old, and I have been in love with technology ever since. After moving away from my fascinations with GameMaker, I started learning Java for my new-found interest in Minecraft. I started playing around with HTML and CSS not long after, and soon enough I had delved into an array of different programming languages.


Today, I spend my free time with the Minecraft community creating new Bukkit / Spigot plugins. I simply do what I enjoy doing, which has led me to pursue a career in the technology field. When I'm not working on a Minecraft project, I can be found hanging out with Tinkerbell (she's my Raspberry Pi), or with my face buried in a book. Currently, I work as a Server Support Specialist at Cubedhost.com, which has been a blast.
