About Me

My name is Haden, better known across the Internet as Cynicalvoe. I am currently an undergraduate university student studying Computer Information Systems and Business Administration in Colorado, USA. Throughout my college career, I have attained a number of Microsoft certifications and real-world experience in the IT industry, coupled with the basic knowledge of how to run a values-aligned business.

Personality

Myers-Briggs: INTJ Enneagram: Type 3 Wing 4

My interest in the IT field began when I was 8 years old, and it quickly evolved from editing home videos to creating computer games and writing code. I sold my first program to a friend when I was 13, and in High School I developed a time-management application for a few of my friends. Beginning in 2012, I started developing Minecraft server mods after teaching myself Java. Some of my favorite Minecraft projects can be found on the Portfolio page for more information.

Today, I work as an online tech support representative for CubedHost.com, and I plan to enter a career in software development when I graduate. When I'm not at my job or doing homework, you can often find me playing tabletop games with friends, watching anime, or hanging out with Tinkerbell (she's my Raspberry Pi).
