About Me

Haden

My name is Haden, better known across the Internet as xFyreWolfx or FireBreath15. I am currently a college student studying Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. When I'm not studying, I spend most of my time programming, playing games, and with my friends. I have been working with code since I was 12 years old, and I have been in love with technology ever since. After moving away from my fascinations with GameMaker, I started learning Java for my new-found interest in Minecraft. I started playing around with HTML and CSS not long after, and the rest, as they say, is history.

Today I am still invested in the Minecraft community as a Bukkit/Spigot plugin developer, as well as a Server Support agent at CubedHost, LLC. I simply do what I enjoy doing, which has led me to pursue a career in the technology field. I hope to one day own my own technology business, or work for a software firm as a software engineer.

When school is not in session, I enjoy spending my time working on exciting new Minecraft plugins, creating something new with my Raspberry Pi, reading and writing sci-fi fiction, keeping up with the latest in white-hat hacking, enjoying the outdoors, and spending time with friends.
