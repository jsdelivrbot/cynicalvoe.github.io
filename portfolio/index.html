My Portfolio

Welcome to my Portfolio! Below are some of my favorite projects, both completed and still in-progress. See the panels below for more information!

Take control of your players. Literally! iControlU is one of my most popular projects, boasting more than 360,000 total downloads as of 2017. Taking control of other players and using their own identities against them has never been easier! The latest version retails on Spigot and can be downloaded on this page. Older versions of the plugin can be found for free on Bukkit on this page.

Nanobots is my first collaboration project, in which its concept was created by myself and a few of my friends. This plugin enables players to craft Nanoblocks. These dastardly creations can devour specific blocks, or eat through just about everything in their place. Explore Grey Goo on your server today! Nanobots can be downloaded for free on Spigot here.

One of my favorite ongoing projects, this plugin provides a server-wide and open-world hacking adventure that is not only easy to install, but it is efficient and powerful. In this game mode, players can "hack" enemy NPC computers and other users to gain experience, in-game currency, and more. Check it out on Spigot!

Who ya gonna call? Introducing Ghostblocks: a simple mechanic that adds a unique twist to any server! These are not only easy to craft, use, and manage, but are efficient and highly customizable. Create disappearing blocks to hide your secret base, trick your friends, or add a sense of adventure to your server. Buy it now on Spigot!

Standing as the pinnacle of society is the Yotsuba Group from Tsugumi Ohba's series Death Note. Correlated closely to my plugin with a similar name, this fan-made website was another collaboration between some friends and I. Aiming to be a realistic representation of the fictional company, this website is a work-in-progress.
