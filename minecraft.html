The Grid


The Grid is my passtime Minecraft server which runs completely on custom software. On The Grid, you take on the role of a hacker, whose only goal is to get as strong as possible. Hack others, find hidden laptops, and upgrade your Viruses to rule the world. The game is set in a French industrial city with beautiful architecture and engaging gameplay. Currently, it is in Beta but is growing exponentially.
