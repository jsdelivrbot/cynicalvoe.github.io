About Me

My name is Haden, better known as xFyreWolfx or FireBreath15. I'm a college student studying Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. When I'm not studying, I spend most of my time programming, playing games, and with my friends.

I have been programming since I was 12 years old, and have fallen in love with technology ever since. After moving away from the introductory Computer Science of GameMaker, I started learning Java for my new-found interest in Minecraft. HTML and CSS came not long after, and before I knew it I had learned a total of 7 different languages altogether. Today I am still invested in the Minecraft community as a Bukkit/Spigot plugin developer, as well as a Server Support agent at CubedHost, LLC.

I simply do what I enjoy doing, which as led me to pursue a career in the technology field. I am currently working to attain a degree in Computer Information Systems, which I hope to pair with my second degree in Business Administration.
