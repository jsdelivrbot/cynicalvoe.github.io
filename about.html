When I'm not hacking on things or writing pieces of code, I enjoy spending time outdoors, hanging out with friends, and playing video games. Oh, and yes - I have a job as well. I currently work at CubedHost, LLC part-time as a Minecraft Server Support Specialist.

Many people ask me why I chose the username xFyreWolfx. Honestly, my usernames change quite often. Another random fact about me is that I consider my personality similar to one of a wolf, and being that my previous username was FireBreath15 I thought I'd combine the two.
